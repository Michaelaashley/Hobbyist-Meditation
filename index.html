Meditation

Let's calm the mind.

Step 1: Relax the body and sit up right either cross-legged on the floor or in a comfortable chair, preferably with a back rest.

Step 2: Once you feel comfortable, choose an object to focus on like a candle, a sleeping pet or you can close you eyes.

Step 3: Pay close attention to your breath. Take a slow deep breath in, hold for 2 seconds and then slowly breathe out.

Step 4: Allow your mind to come to a quiet pause for 30 to 60 minutes. This takes time and practice so don’t worry if it’s hard to do at first. Practice makes perfect!

Step 5: That’s it! once you make this a routine every day you’ll start to feel more relaxed and enlightened. This isn’t a sprint but a marathon so be easy on yourself and have fun!
